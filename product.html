Your All-in-one, AI-Powered Field Service Operations Platform

Automate scheduling, enable mobile technicians, and deliver exceptional customer service with Zinier's AI-powered platform. From real-time dispatching to intelligent work order management, streamline your entire field service operation from a single, configurable platform.

Features

24/7 Instant Response to Service Requests

Resolve issues faster with an AI-powered chat that answers customer inquiries day and night, books appointments in real time, and keeps everyone informed with automated status updates.

AI-powered Chat

Answers customer inquiries day and night, providing instant support without requiring human agents to be available around the clock.

Frictionless, Self-service Appointment Booking

Customers can schedule appointments via the Customer Portal with real-time availability, eliminating back-and-forth scheduling calls.

Clear Technician Arrival and Operational Visibility

Real-time location tracking, ETA visibility, automated status notifications, and dispatcher visibility keep customers informed every step of the way.

Capacity Management

Balance workload and skills to ensure deliverable appointments, preventing over-booking and optimizing technician utilization.

Get It Right the First Time

Auto-scheduling puts the best-matched technician on every job, while our mobile app and AI support ensure the job is done right the first time.

Mobile App (Task Execution, Forms & Checklists)

Guided mobile experience with smart fault resolution powered by LLMs, ensuring technicians have everything they need at their fingertips.

Automated Scheduling

Configurable auto-scheduler for best-matched technician assignment based on skills, location, availability, and job requirements.

Inventory & Parts Management

Real-time inventory and vehicle stock visibility alignment ensures technicians arrive with the right parts for the job.

Documentation Agent

AI trained on OEM manuals and work instructions provides instant answers to technical questions in the field.

AI Voice Radio

Hands-free voice agents for job preparation and query answering, allowing technicians to stay productive while working.

Scale Without Scaling Headcount

Take on more work without hiring more technicians, coordinators or support staff. One manager can run a much larger field team.

Turn Customer Touch-points into Revenue Opportunities

Let customers request new installations, services, and upgrades through an AI-powered chat interface — automatically converting customer touch-points into booked revenue. Technicians can request new installations, additional services and product upgrades on site, turning a site visit into additional revenue.

AI Voice to Form

Technicians can quickly fill in forms that took hours in minutes using only their voice, allowing them to complete more jobs in less time.

Automate with Recommendations

Recommendations automate and mimic the logic a seasoned dispatcher might apply — allowing fewer coordinators to manage larger field teams.

Deliver Consistent Service Fit to How Your Team Operates

Configure tasks, workflows, roles, and reporting to match your operations — without code — so teams adopt faster, work more efficiently, and deliver consistently as you scale.

Workforce Management (Shifts, Regions, Crews and More)

Structure your workforce by regions, crews, shifts, and working hours to mirror how teams operate in the field — enabling smarter scheduling, clearer accountability, and scalable growth.

Configurable Timesheets

Track working hours, overtime, and job time using timesheets configured to your team's actual work patterns without manual effort.

No-code Form Builder

Design and update mobile forms/checklists using a no-code, drag-and-drop builder — so technicians follow consistent, best-practice steps without slowing down field work or relying on IT.

Dashboard Builder

Create role-based dashboards that surface the KPIs that matter most — so owners, dispatchers, and managers can spot issues early and optimize performance in real time.
